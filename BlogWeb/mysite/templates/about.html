Our Culinary Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming your favorite food destination, our story is seasoned with passion and served with love

How It All Started

In 2010, chef Marco Bianchi began cooking meals for his neighbors from his small apartment kitchen. What began as a passion project quickly grew into a local sensation. Today, FoodExpress delivers over 10,000 meals daily across the city, but we still maintain that home-cooked quality in every dish.

Our secret? We never compromise on ingredients. Every tomato, every herb, every slice of meat is personally selected by our team of culinary experts.
